Information on Bed Bugs

Latin Name

Cimex Lectularius

Interesting Fact About Bed Bugs

Bed bugs have small, flat, oval-shaped bodies. They are wingless. Adults do have the vestiges of wings called wing pads, but they do not fully develop into functional wings. Newly hatched often are described as being about the size and shape of an apple seed.

Treatment:

Dry heat treatment is an effective method of controlling bed bugs. This treatment is only conducted by a Pest Management Professional in order to ensure that infested items and rooms reach the required temperature for effective pest control.
